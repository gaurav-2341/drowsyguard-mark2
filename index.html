  • 100% Client-side – no data leaves your device

How to Get Started

  1. Train your model on Google Teachable Machine (Image Project).
  2. Export as TensorFlow.js and copy your model.json link.
  3. Open the Monitor page, paste your link, and click Start.
  4. Allow camera access and keep the browser in view.

Tip: Works best with good lighting and a stable camera view of your face.
